About me

I'm Nischal, a self-taught full-stack developer based in Kathmandu, Nepal. I started building real-world projects during secondary school — from e-commerce platforms to hospital management systems — and turned that into freelance income on Upwork before most people my age had written their first function.

My core stack spans Django REST Framework, React, PostgreSQL, and JavaScript, with hands-on experience in JWT authentication, Docker, REST API design, and CI/CD pipelines. I'm currently deepening my JavaScript expertise through The Odin Project while pursuing a BSc in Computer Science, bridging self-taught instincts with formal foundations.

I'm drawn to large-scale systems and the kind of engineering problems that don't have clean answers. I build carefully, ship deliberately, and care about the details that most people skip.

Currently open to remote junior developer roles.
